Here are the stand adjustment instructions for the **Wacom Cintiq Pro 27**:

---

## Assembly

1. Place a soft cloth on a flat surface and lay your Cintiq Pro screen-down on top.
2. Screw the **pivot plate** into the 4 VESA mounting holes on the back of the display.
3. Slide arm buttons through the base plate holes (buttons at the top of the holes).
4. Screw the base plate into the arm.
5. Remove the mounting cap from the arm, slide the pivot plate onto the arm, then screw in the fixing bracket and replace the cap.
6. Route cables behind the stand using the included cable ties.

---

## Tilt / Height Adjustment (up to 60°)

1. **Unlock** — Push the arm lock **down**.
2. **Adjust** — Tilt the screen to your preferred angle (up to 60°).
3. **Lock** — Push the arm lock **up** to secure the position.

---

## Rotation Adjustment (up to 40°)

1. Push the rotation switch **down** to enable rotation.
2. Rotate the screen up to 40°.
3. Push the rotation switch **up** when returning to the upright position.

---

## Lift Strength Fine-Tuning

If the arm doesn't hold position well:
- **Too heavy** (arm can't hold at highest position) or **too light** (rises from a low position): raise the arm to its highest position and adjust the **arm lift strength screw** (may require many turns).
- If the front edge of the display doesn't stay up when raised, tighten the screw under the display attachment point.

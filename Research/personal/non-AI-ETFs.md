## Commentary

### Why Mid-Cap?
- **No mega-cap concentration:** Top holdings average 1-3% weight, vs. 5-7%+ in broad-market funds
- **Tesla-free by construction:** Mid-cap range ($2B–$10B) excludes all mega-caps
- **Growth exposure without bubble risk:** Mid-caps capture tech innovation cycle 3–7 years behind mega-caps, with lower valuations
- **Downside protection:** When mega-cap multiples compress, mid-cap growth still holds fundamentals

## What This Strategy Does NOT Do

⚠️ **Important caveat:** This allocation *lags* the broad market when mega-cap tech outperforms (e.g., 2023–2024). You're accepting 10+ year underperformance if AI adoption accelerates and mega-caps remain dominant. This is a *hedge trade*, not a performance bet.

If you're uncertain about bubble timing, consider 60/40 split: 60% broad market (VTI), 40% mid-cap focus. That keeps you indexed to mega-cap growth while hedging downturn.

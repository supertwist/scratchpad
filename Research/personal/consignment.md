## 4. Gallery’s Responsibilities

1. **Display & Promotion** – Exhibit the Artwork in a prominent location and include it in regular promotional activities (press releases, catalogs, website, social media, exhibition catalogs, etc.).
2. **Security & Insurance** – Maintain reasonable security. The Gallery shall insure the Artwork for its full retail value against loss, theft, or damage while in its possession, naming the Artist as an additional insured.

---

## 5. Artist’s Responsibilities

1. Deliver the Artwork in **sale‑ready condition**, properly packed and labeled with the unique identifier.
2. Provide accurate provenance, authenticity documentation, and any required certificates of authenticity.
3. Pay any applicable customs or export duties if the Artwork is shipped from outside the Gallery’s jurisdiction.

---

## 6. Pricing & Commission

1. **Retail Price** – The Artist sets the retail price for each piece (see Exhibit A). Any price adjustments must be approved in writing by the Artist.
2. **Gallery Commission** – The Gallery shall retain **______ %** of the gross sales price of each Artwork sold.
3. **Artist’s Net Proceeds** – The remaining **______ %** (after deducting the Gallery’s commission and any agreed‑upon expenses) shall be paid to the Artist.

---

## 7. Sales Procedure

- Upon sale, the Gallery will provide the Artist with a sales receipt showing: buyer’s name, sale price, commission, and net amount due.
- **Payment Terms:** The Gallery shall remit the Artist’s net proceeds within **30 days** of receipt of payment from the buyer, via electronic transfer or check, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

---

## 8. Title & Ownership

Title to the Artwork remains with the Artist until a sale is consummated. The Gallery holds the Artwork **solely in trust** for the Artist and shall return any unsold pieces, at the Artist’s request, at the end of the consignment period.

---

## 9. Return of Unsold Artwork

The Artist may request the return of any or all unsold Artwork at any time. The Gallery shall, within **10 business days** of such request, package and ship the Artwork back at the Artist’s expense (unless otherwise agreed).

---

## 10. Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement with **30 days** written notice. Upon termination, the Gallery shall promptly return all unsold Artwork and any related documentation to the Artist.

---

## 11. Indemnification

Each Party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other from any claims, losses, or damages arising out of its own breach of this Agreement, negligence, or willful misconduct.

---
